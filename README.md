Voice of Prophecy LMS

VOP LMS is a web-based platform developed by Kellzman Tech Ltd to support the Voice of Prophecy initiative within the SDA Church.
It enables structured online Bible study and training through permanent course modules, quizzes, reports, and certificate generation.
